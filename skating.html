--- layout: page title: Skating. permalink: /skating/ --- I began learning how to skate when I was three and competed in singles up to the novice level. After, I switched to synchronized skating and competed on Team Lower Mainland Junior, representing BC at the Canadian National Skating Championships. I stopped skating because of university, but realized that I missed it and wanted to return. I joined Team Nova Junior, placing third at Nationals and representing Canada at international Challenger Series competitions. I am a NCCP Trained Regional Coach and am currently working on my portfolio to become fully certified.
