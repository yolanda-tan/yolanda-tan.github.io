I began learning how to skate when I was three and competed in singles up to the novice level. I've had the opportunity to compete at the BC Winter Games and have been a provincial medalist twice.

After, I switched to synchronized skating and represented BC at the Canadian National Skating Championships. Most recently, I've competed as part of Team Nova Junior, placing third at Nationals and representing Canada at international Challenger Series competitions. I am also a NCCP Trained Regional Coach currently in the processes of completing my portfolio to become fully certified.
